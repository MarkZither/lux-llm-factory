# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation.
- Be considerate of different backgrounds and perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or repeated disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:

- coc@zither-it.co.uk

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement

Enforcement guidelines are not finalized yet.
Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary restriction, or removal from participation.
